## Overview

The Premium command displays a comprehensive comparison of free vs Pro Engine limits for all bot systems. Use it to see what features are available at each tier and what you unlock by upgrading.

## What You'll See

<Callout type="info">
  - **Feature Comparison** — Side-by-side free vs Pro limits - **Upgrade
  Instructions** — How to purchase Cores and activate Pro Engine - **Voting
  Rewards** — How to earn free Cores by voting on top.gg
</Callout>

## Usage

```bash
/premium
```

No additional parameters required.

## Pro Engine Pricing

Pro Engine costs **20 Cores per week**. You can purchase Cores on the [website](https://rolereactor.app) or earn them for free by voting.
