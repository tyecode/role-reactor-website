## Step-by-Step Example
1. Generate a basic avatar:
   ```bash
   /avatar prompt:cyberpunk hacker with neon hair
   ```
2. Generate with style options:
   ```bash
   /avatar prompt:kawaii girl with pink cat ears color_style:vibrant mood:cute art_style:chibi
   ```
3. The bot will generate your unique anime-style avatar using AI.

## Features
- **AI-Powered Generation:** Uses advanced AI models to create unique anime-style avatars
- **Multiple Styles:** Choose from Studio Ghibli, Manga, Modern, Retro, Realistic, Chibi, or Lo-fi styles
- **Color Palettes:** Vibrant, Pastel, Monochrome, Neon, Warm, or Cool color schemes
- **Mood Options:** Happy, Serious, Mysterious, Cute, Cool, or Elegant expressions
- **Custom Prompts:** Describe exactly what you want in your avatar
- **Core Credit System:** Uses 1 Core credit per generation (check balance with `/core balance`)

## Troubleshooting
<Callout type="warning">
- **Insufficient credits:** Check your Core balance with `/core balance`. Purchase credits via Ko-fi or subscribe to a membership tier.<br />
- **Generation failed:** The AI service may be temporarily unavailable. Try again in a few moments.<br />
- **Prompt too long:** Keep prompts under 500 characters for best results.<br />
- **Content filtered:** Some prompts may be filtered for safety. Try rephrasing your request.
</Callout>
