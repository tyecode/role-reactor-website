## Advanced Customization
- Add more subjects or group types as your server grows.
- Use custom emoji for school branding or mascot.
- Create separate role messages for courses, clubs, or events.

## Troubleshooting
<Callout type="warning">
- **Roles not assigned:** Check bot permissions and role order.
- **Emoji not working:** Use emoji the bot can access.
- **Members unsure:** Provide a pinned guide for what each role means.
</Callout>

## Best Practices
<Callout type="success">
- Group roles by subject and study style.<br />
- Encourage users to join relevant groups for collaboration.<br />
- Use clear emoji for each subject or group.<br />
- Update roles each semester or term.
</Callout>
